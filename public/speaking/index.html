How can Rohit help you as a speaker?

For Schools
Our education system surprisingly misses some key components of a child's education when it comes to preparing her for a happy successful life. There are some simple principles which when understood and applied can lead to surprising academic results and also lead to holistic development of a child. Rohit can act as an instrument to bring these principles to life at you school.
For Coaching Institutions
A coaching's success is measured by the selection ratio of its students. In this world of cut-throat competition your students need to be better prepared than anyone else. Along with studying, they need to learn all the skills needed for success. They need to have the right attitude and the right belief system if they want to succeed. Rohit can help your students take leaps where others are only walking.
For Colleges
Today, students are very intelligent but don't know what to do with it. They are full of energy but waste it on unproductive endeavours. They want to be successful but don't understand what it takes to get there. They need somebody to guide them, to bring clarity of thought so that they can be what they want to be and do what they want to do. Rohit can help your students get that clarity and create a world-class life.
