How can Rohit help you as a speaker?

For Students
All Children are born into greatness but they don't know how to manifest it. They have the required ingredients of creativity, confidence, sincerity and innocence but cooking success is a different story all together. This is where Rohit comes in. Rohit's workshop has the sublime effect of bringing children in touch with their inner self. He brings clarity to their minds while making them laugh. He teaches them how to channelize their energy to get what they want. He shows them a way to make their dreams come true. Getting them a session with Rohit is one of the best things you can do for your children's success
For Teacher
It is not the constant pestering of teachers that leads to education but the inner curiosity of a child. But How to do that? How to inspire a child to learn? How to kindle that fire of finding answers? Rohit believes that a teacher's job is not to show the students something but to teach them how to see. He can train your teachers to do the same. He can coach them to make the simple attitude and belief system shifts which lead to better class participation and improved learning outcomes. Booking a workshop with Rohit is one of the best things you can do for the betterment of the education quality in your school.
For Parents
Children don't do what you say, they do what you do. Parents are a child's biggest influencers. However many a time, parents are unaware of the kind of influence they are for their child. All parents want their children to be happy and successful but only a few are preparing them to be. Great Parenting is a skill to be learnt in the modern day and age. There is no stopping a child whose parents understand their role in her holistic development. The school can be a platform to coach the parents regarding this. It fulfils your objective of moulding the best in class human being from your school. Book a workshop with Rohit to do just that.
