How can Rohit help you as a speaker?

For Students
The measure of the quality of a college is how well it prepares a student for what's coming in life. We all know that success depends on not only domain knowledge but also soft skills. Sometimes, how confident you are matters more than what you know. But our education system is such that seldom do students learn how to cultivate these soft skills. This is where Rohit comes in. Rohit's sessions are a hilarious streak of deep insights. He opens up a student's mind to the simple yet deep truths of life that are essential for success. He makes them realise their true potential and shows them how to use it. A session with Rohit is the best thing you can do for your children.
For Teachers
A Teacher has been given the highest position in a student's life for the sole reason that the teacher possesses the power to shape a student's future. The task of a college teacher is particularly difficult considering the fact that students in a college don't want to listen to their teachers. Despite being shining lights in their respective fields, teacher often miss out on the basics of mind management which plays a vital role in the success of a child. This is where Rohit comes in. A session with Rohit can bring a sea change in the perspective of your teachers and make them more caring and skilled to handle the vagaries of the young mind. A session with Rohit is one of the best things you can do for your teachers and the growth of your institution.
